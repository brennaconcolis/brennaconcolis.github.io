Research

I am currently pursuing a PhD at the University of Hamburg with a focus on marine extreme events. My research is based at the Helmholtz-Zentrum Hereon, where I work under the Department of Climate Extremes and Impacts.

Research Interests

  • Marine heatwaves and cold spells
  • Climate variability and extreme weather
  • Modeling ocean-atmosphere interactions

Current Projects

  • Analyzing marine heatwaves in the North Sea using reanalysis data
  • Using machine learning techniques for forecasting marine heatwaves
